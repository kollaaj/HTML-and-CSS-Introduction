About Brún

Brún restaurant is situated in a 160 year old building in the centre of Reykjavík. We like to think that Food is for your body and Music is for your soul.

The food is in the Icelandic brasserie style of food, elegantly prepared by our skilled and enthusiastic chefs, using the Icelandic finest ingredients.
